About NETCO II

NETCO (Network of Cities for Collaborative Housing) promotes peer learning and exchange among city practitioners, civil society and organisations working on collective and collaborative housing.

NETCO II is the renewed phase of the project. NETCO I materials remain available in the archive.

  • 01 How the network works

    The project is structured in Working Packages led by partners, supported by coordination and shared publication on the website.

  • 02 Formats

    Webinars, in-person network meetings and focus groups will be used for knowledge exchange and peer learning.
