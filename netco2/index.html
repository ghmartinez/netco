About NETCO II

NETCO II continues the Network of Cities for Collaborative Housing, reinforcing peer learning and cooperation among cities, civil society, and organisations working on collective housing approaches at local level.

  • More planning and structure than the previous phase
  • Stronger management, tracking, and coordination across the network
  • Clear communication support so partners know “who does what”

NETCO II strengthens how the network operates: closer collaboration with partners leading each work stream, shared communication resources, and better coordination around deliverables.

This phase focuses on practical exchange across cities and partners, improving shared visibility of activities and outcomes, and building reusable communication workflows for the network.

Work Packages & Formats

NETCO II includes 10 Work Packages implemented through three main formats. Communication support is designed so that partners leading each Work Package can easily publish, share and amplify activities across the network.

Webinars

Online sessions to share practices, policy tools and implementation lessons. (Partner-led; webinars are facilitated by Urbamonde.)

Status: Calendar coming soon.

Network Meetings

In-person meetings gathering the network to consolidate learning, align on outputs and reinforce connections between partners.

Status: Dates coming soon.

Focus Groups

Smaller working groups focused on specific topics, tools or deliverables, with tighter collaboration between partners.

Status: Topics and schedule coming soon.

Communication support model: partners leading each activity share a minimal set of inputs (title, date, speakers, links, visuals) and the coordination team supports publication on the website and shared distribution across channels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Key questions about NETCO II and how the network works.

  • What is NETCO II?

    NETCO II is the current phase of the Network of Cities for Collaborative Housing. It reinforces coordination, follow-up and shared communication support for partners leading activities and work packages.

  • How are activities organised?

    NETCO II operates through 10 work packages implemented via three formats: webinars, network meetings, and focus groups. Partners lead activities and the coordination team supports publication and distribution.
