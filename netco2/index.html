About NETCO II

Network of Cities for Collaborative Housing (NETCO) is a network of cities and partners collaborating to exchange practices and strengthen local policies on collaborative housing. NETCO II is the renewed project phase, with increased coordination and clearer shared working routines.

NETCO II is structured around 10 Working Packages and the three main working formats described above.
