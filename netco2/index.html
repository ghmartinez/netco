NETCO II — Coming Soon

NETCO II is the second phase of the NETCO Project. In this edition we will strengthen coordination and hands-on communication support for partners leading the Working Packages, with more planning, structure, and regular follow-up than in the previous phase.

This page is being updated. The calendar, materials, partner directory and communication resources will be published shortly.

What will be published here

  • Activity calendar (webinars, full-network in-person meetings, and smaller focus groups)
  • Communication materials and templates (slides, docs, visual assets)
  • Partner directory (partners, links and social profiles)
  • Reporting and communication deliverables
  • Basic communication protocol (who provides what, and who publishes what, for each format)

Working formats

  • Webinars (online sessions)
  • In-person network meetings (full consortium gatherings)
  • Focus groups (smaller working sessions)
